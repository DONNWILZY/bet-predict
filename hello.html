Wellness in The Park

Free Community Fitness & Wellness Events Across London

Your health. Your neighborhood. Your time to move.

Join us every Saturday for outdoor yoga, aerobics, and wellness sessions - powered by local coaches, wearable tech, and good vibes. 👉 No sign-up fees. Just show up with a smile and your gear.

What to Expect

  • 🌿 Dynamic Warm-Ups & Mobility Drills
  • 💃🏽 High-Energy Aerobics or Dance Cardio
  • 🧘🏾‍♂️ Flow-Based Yoga or Stretch Sessions
  • 💬 Short Wellness Talks (hydration, posture, stress relief)
  • 🎶 Live music or curated playlists to keep you moving
